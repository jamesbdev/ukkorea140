2023 marks the 140th anniversary of establishing diplomatic relations between the UK and Korea. To celebrate this occasion, we invite you all to participate in our video contest and become part of history!

This past decade has seen an incredible increase in cultural exchanges and travel between our two nations. We have seen the Hallyu wave sweep across the UK and the impact of British TV and cinema in Korea. It is not just entertainment and art that strengthens our bond, but more so the interest and fascination with our respective cultures. What unites us can only make us stronger!

Now, here is how you can become part of history!
We want you to create a video to celebrate this anniversary and be in a chance to win some fantastic prizes.

Topics

  1. Introduce Korean & British Culture to Your Friends
  2. Your Korean and British Experiences

You have creative freedom when creating your video. Whether you want to introduce your Korean or British friends, talk about your life in the UK or Korea, discuss your travels and cultural exploration or even talk about the community itself, we want to know all about it!'

If you are struggling with ideas, below are a few examples to help you

  • Cultural Experiences: British/Korean Food, Music, Movies, Taekwondo, Instruments, Art, Kpop, Kbeauty etc.
  • A moment in your life: Vlog, travels in the UK/Korea, introducing your friends, introducing a community/class you attend etc.
  • Documentary type, music video, movie, animation, performance and vlog are all acceptable submissions as long as they are your own footage.자유롭게 여러분의 이야기를 영상으로 만들어주세요.
